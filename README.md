📖Introduction:
**City of Horror** is a **cooperative** experience that puts players in the role of the city's heroes, who must band together and plan to stop the monsters from advancing.
With a graphical and user-friendly environment

The city is in a state of panic. Monsters roam the streets and the fear is growing by the minute, but you are not alone.

**City of Terror** is a cooperative game where a group of heroes come together to:
- Defeat monsters using **Score Cards** and **Item Cards**.
- Use **Team Coordination** to save the villagers.
- Control the **Fear Level** and restore peace to the city.

⚠️ Warning: If fear gets out of control, the city will fall forever.

🖥️ Features:
- Cooperative and team gameplay
- Scoring system and smart cards
- Attractive and user-oriented graphical environment
- Number of users: 2-4
